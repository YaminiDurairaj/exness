CFDs are complex derivative products which are traded outside an exchange. These products come with a high risk of losing money rapidly due to leverage and thus are not appropriate for all investors.

*The term "instant" indicates that a transaction is carried out within a few seconds without manual processing by financial department specialists. This does not guarantee that a deposit and withdrawal will be completed instantaneously, but that the process is begun instantly. Processing times depend entirely on the payment method chosen.
